HOW TO IMPORT "TutorialProjectFiles.unitypackage" TO YOUR UNITY PROJECT

1. Open "Unity Tutorial - Reflection Probe" using Unity 2017.2 or up.
2. Inside "_Scene" folder open "Scene_01" scene.
3. The scene should look too bright and washed out, to fix that go to "Edit > Project Settings > Player" and set "Color Space*" to "Linear".

-You can control the Sphere using W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys.
-You can also jump with a Green cube using Space.
